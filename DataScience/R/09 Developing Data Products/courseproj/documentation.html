This application help you to investigate the daily return and daily volume of the two selected markets

  1. NASDAQ Composite (Yahoo symbol: ^IXIC)
  2. Heng Seng Index (Yahoo symbol: ^HSI)

The data is from 1 July, 2010 to 30 June, 2015.

User can select the market from the left side panel and also the date range, and the histogram of daily return and daily volume is plotted. Mean and standard deviation is also shown at the top of the histogram.
